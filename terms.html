1. What Luca is

Luca is a shopping assistant for dog owners. Luca is not a retailer. We do not sell products, hold inventory, or fulfill orders. When you approve a cart, Luca directs you to third-party retailer websites (such as Chewy, Amazon, Walmart, PetSmart, Petco, Costco, Target, and Tractor Supply) where you complete your purchase directly with that retailer.
