How It Works

Three steps. Two minutes to go live.

01

Set your rules

Configure your max discount, minimum floor, and crash probability weights. You control exactly how generous every session is — bracket by bracket.

02

Shoppers play

A "Reveal Your Discount" button appears on the cart. The meter climbs toward your max — but it could crash at any moment. The tension is the product.

03

They cash out

Shoppers lock in their discount whenever they want. A unique Shopify discount code is generated and auto-applied at checkout. No coupon codes to type.

Quick Setup

Go live in under two minutes

No developers. No code. Just install, configure, and enable.

  1. Install Anticipation Engine from the Shopify App Store.
  2. Open the AE dashboard in your Shopify admin. Set your max discount, min floor, and crash weights.
  3. Go to Online Store → Themes → Customize. Navigate to your Cart page.
  4. Add the Reveal My Price block from Apps in the sidebar. Position it near your checkout button.
  5. Hit Save. You're live.

Key configuration options

Maximum Discount
The highest percentage a shopper can potentially reach. This is the ceiling — most will cash out below it.
Recommended: 15–25%
Minimum Floor
Where the meter starts. Shoppers always begin here when they click reveal.
Recommended: 5–10%
Crash Weights
Control crash probability across 5% brackets. Front-load weights to lower brackets to keep your average discount low while maintaining excitement.
e.g. 40% / 30% / 20% / 10%
Cooldown & Volume Cap
Prevent repeat plays and cap daily sessions to control total discount exposure.
