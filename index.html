How it works

Three steps. More time for the dog park.

1

Tell Luca about your dog

Breed, age, weight, allergies, diet — a 2-minute quiz that powers everything. Multi-dog households welcome.

2

Luca builds your cart

Every week, Luca compares prices on food, treats, toys, supplements, and grooming supplies across 8 retailers — and drafts a cart with the best deals on everything.

3

Approve and save

Open the app, glance at the cart, remove anything you don't want, and tap approve. That's your entire job.

Cross-retailer price optimization

Food, treats, toys, supplements, grooming supplies — Luca checks Chewy, Amazon, Walmart, Costco, PetSmart, Petco, Target, and Tractor Supply for the cheapest option on every item.

Recall monitoring

Luca scans the FDA database daily. If anything in your dog's cart gets recalled — food, treats, chews — it's removed and replaced before you hear about it.

Smart substitutions

Out of stock? Recalled? Overpriced? Luca finds the closest replacement that respects your dog's allergies, preferences, and nutritional needs.

Restock predictions

Luca learns how fast your dog goes through everything — food, treats, supplements, shampoo — and adds items to your cart before you run out.
